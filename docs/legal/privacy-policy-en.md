## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Data processed locally (on your device)

This data **never leaves your phone**:

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Your voice | Transcription to text | Not stored (real-time processing) |
| Transcription | Executing your commands | Session duration |
| Notifications | Reading at your request | Not stored by Diva |
| Contacts | Sending messages | Read-only access |
| Calendar | Creating reminders | Read/write access |

### 3.2 Data sent to cloud (optional)

If you enable cloud processing for complex requests:

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Anonymized transcription | Advanced understanding | 0 (ephemeral, not stored) |
| Conversation context | Continuity | 24 hours maximum |

**You can disable cloud processing** in the app settings. Diva will work in 100% local mode with reduced capabilities.

### 3.3 Account data

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Email | Identification | Until account deletion |
| Preferences | Personalization | Until account deletion |

## 7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

| Right | Description | How to exercise |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Access** | Get a copy of your data | Settings > My Data > Export |
| **Rectification** | Correct your data | Settings > Profile |
| **Erasure** | Delete your data | Settings > Delete my account |
| **Restriction** | Suspend processing | Settings > Pause mode |
| **Portability** | Retrieve your data | Settings > My Data > Export |
| **Objection** | Refuse certain processing | Settings > Privacy |
| **Withdraw consent** | Cancel your consent | Settings > Permissions |

To exercise these rights by email: privacy@[domain].com

We will respond within 30 days.
